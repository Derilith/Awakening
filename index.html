Here we vote on Your favorite/best villans of all time.

Sheev Palpatine

Media/Univirse: Star Wars

Titles/Ocupation: Emperor, senator, chancellor, Sith Lord

Description: Palpatine was the Emperor of the Galactic Empire and a dark lord of the Sith. Cunning, manipulitive and at times egomaniacal, he rose to power by outwitting his enemeis and at times utilizing them in his grand plan.

Gul Dukat

Media/Univirse: Star trek Deep Space 9

Titles/Ocupation: Gul, Prefect, Legate.

Description: Dukat was the prefect of Bajor during the Cardassian Unions occupation, a horrific affair where the local populace where used as slaves to strip mine the planet and rob its spiritual people of all their resources. He as a man was ruthless and cunning, always planing how he could achive a higher rank in the society in which he was raised and loves. He could however be kind, generous and honorable, when it suited his purposes. Dukat would form dangerous alliances and eventually betray the entire galaxy in order to prove he was better than his rivals.

Baron Harkonnen

Media/Univirse: Dune

Titles/Ocupation: Baron of house Harkonnen

Description: Although one cannot really say any of the great houses in the Dune Universe are good, one thing can most certainly be said, Baron Harkonnen is unequivocally evil. A man infected by what seems to be every disease known to man, he is not above murder, assasination, bribery, extortion, black mail, or even canabalism. He is a disgusting blaoted man, unable to move without assistance and constantly being cared for. Always plotting his next move to become the greatest of the great houses.

Sovereign

Media/Univirse: Mass Effect

Titles/Ocupation: Reaper

Description: Once thought to be a massively powerful and ancient Warship, Sovereign was in fact a member of an extramly advanced race of space fairing non-organic life forms bent on wiping out all advanced intelligence species in the galaxy. Powerful, manipulative and capable of feats even the most advaced warships in the galaxy could not hope to preform. Even just being in the vicinity of this monstrosity could sway you to its side, through the use of an indoctrination feild.

Maleficent

Media/Univirse: Disnery

Titles/Ocupation: sorceress.

Description: The incarnation of pure evil, Maleficent is an amazingly powerful being, capable of useing destructive magics, as well as more sinister ones. She has even been known to turn herself into a massive firebreathing Dragon.

Sauron

Media/Univirse: The Lord of the Rings

Titles/Ocupation: Dark lord, Ringmaker.

Description: Sauron is a being filled with malice and hatred, a being so powerful he almost took complete and total control of Middle Earth. Physically towering and immensely strong, with the ability to take out swaths of enemy troops. Immortal and incapable of being truly destroyed, Saurons influence hangs over Middle Earth like a malicious cloud.
